Mountain

Ijen Crater is a crater originating from Mount Ijen and has a number of interesting panoramas for you to visit. Offers natural scenery that can give a sense of serenity for visitors. Even though you have to go through a fairly difficult climb to get there, you won't lose anything once you reach the top. Because you can see the beauty of Blue Fire. This is a view of the blue fire coming from the Ijen crater. You can only see this phenomenon when it is dark or at night.
